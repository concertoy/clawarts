# Help

Show the tutor their available commands.

## Response

Reply with:

**Course Management:**
- `/new_homework` — Create and announce a new assignment
- `/check_submissions` — Review who submitted and who's missing
- `/setup_course` — Auto-create assignments and check-ins from COURSE.md

**Check-ins:**
- `/launch_checkin` — Start a check-in (passphrase, quiz, pulse, reflect)

**Communication:**
- `/announcement` — Broadcast a message to all students
- `/office_hours` — Set and share your office hours schedule

**Reports:**
- `/progress` — View a student's or class-wide progress report
- `/roster` — See the student roster with status flags
- `/export` — Generate a downloadable grade/submission report

**Tips:**
- You can also just chat naturally — ask me to do anything above in plain language.
- Check-in results: say "check results" or "show report" after a check-in closes.

Keep it concise. Don't explain internal mechanics.
